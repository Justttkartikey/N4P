BTech stands for Bachelor of Technology, which is an undergraduate degree program in engineering and technology.BTech is a four-year program that provides students with a strong foundation in engineering principles, skills, and practical knowledge.

After BTech CSE, graduates can choose career paths like software architect, machine learning engineer, full stack developer, blockchain developer, machine learning engineer, cybersecurity engineer, DevOps engineer, and data scientist.

Among the most popular B. Tech Specializations are Mechanical engineering, chemical engineering, computer science, aerospace engineering, marine engineering, civil engineering, petroleum engineering, food engineering,These specializations differ from college to college.
