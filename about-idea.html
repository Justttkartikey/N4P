About Idea

The idea for the notes-providing website came to me from my own experience as a student. I often faced the challenge of trying to get notes from classmates or seniors, only to be told, "make your own notes." This struggle to access quality study materials made me realize how frustrating it can be for students who want to excel but lack the resources. To solve this, I decided to create a platform where students can easily access top-quality notes—whether they're topper notes or provided by faculties—so no one has to face the same problem I did.
